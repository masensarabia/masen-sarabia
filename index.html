Professional Experience

I currently work as an Enterprise Technical Success Manager at ObservePoint, where I manage end-to-end client onboarding processes, lead contract negotiations, identify and pursue upsell opportunities, and effectively resolve complex technical issues. My role is centered on helping clients optimize their use of our web auditing tool, ensuring they extract maximum value from their subscription while also discovering additional features that enhance their experience.

ObservePoint works by visiting thousands of web pages, allowing them to fully load, and then downloading the content as a HAR (HTTP Archive) file. This file captures all data related to the page load, including network requests, file sizes, load times, cookies, and JavaScript activity. The extracted data is then structured into intuitive dashboards, enabling users to monitor site performance, validate analytics implementations, and configure alerts for any identified issues.

One of the aspects I find most rewarding about my role is the ability to add custom JavaScript to audits. This allows us to perform specific checks that go beyond the platform’s default capabilities. I collaborate with clients to brainstorm solutions, and then we integrate custom scripts into their existing audits. These scripts help automate more of their day-to-day tasks, collect additional data beyond the HAR file, and achieve their business goals.

I have successfully managed a portfolio of key enterprise clients, including industry leaders like Google, AT&T, General Motors, Salesforce, Epson, PGA Tour, Capital One, and Mary Kay, to name a few. My responsibilities include collaborating with diverse teams such as Digital Analysts, Marketers, and IT experts to automate workflows and ensure the seamless operation of our solutions. Additionally, I have led initiatives to improve privacy compliance validation processes, significantly enhancing the accuracy and reliability of our platform's results.

I’ve also organized and led customer collaboration calls, bringing together over 20 major clients to share their experiences and best practices around privacy compliance. These calls have been incredibly valuable for our customer base, fostering knowledge-sharing and strengthening client retention.
